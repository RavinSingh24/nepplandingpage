NoVA Extracurricular Pyramid Program

Pushing students to strive for competitive excellence.

About NEPP

The NoVA Extracurricular Pyramid Program (NEPP) is a program that aims to provide mentorship and advice for middle and high school students relating to Model United Nations, Technology Student Association, Science Olympiad, and Debate.
